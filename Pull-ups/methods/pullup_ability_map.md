# Pull-up → Muscle-up Ability Map

Active Hang ↓ Scapular Pull ↓ Strict Pull-up 5 ↓ Strict Pull-up 8 ↓
Chest-to-Bar Pull-up ↓ High Pull ↓ Transition ↓ Muscle-up

## Stage 1 -- Pull-up 5 → 8

Focus: - Strength endurance - Top range strength

Key training: - Pull-up volume - Negative pull-ups - Top hold

Recommended weekly volume: 50--60 reps

## Stage 2 -- Pull-up 8 → 10

Problem: Most athletes cannot pull high enough.

Add: - Chest-to-Bar - Explosive Pull-up

Example training: Pull-up 5×5\
Chest-to-Bar 4×3\
Explosive Pull-up 4×2

Goal: - Pull-up ≥10 - Chest-to-Bar ≥5

## Stage 3 -- High Pull

Goal: Pull to lower chest.

Training: High Pull 5×2

Focus: - Explosive movement - Tight core - Fast pull

## Stage 4 -- Transition

Training: Low Bar Transition\
Band Muscle-up

Structure: 5×3

Key technique: - Lean forward - Wrist turnover - Elbows above bar

## Stage 5 -- Muscle-up

Typical requirement:

Pull-up ≥10\
Dip ≥15\
Chest-to-Bar ≥5
