# Core Training Manual v3

Daily Core System for **L-Sit + Pull-up Stability**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Overview

Goal:

-   Unlock **L-sit**
-   Improve **pull-up body rigidity**
-   Build **gymnastics-style core strength**

Training frequency

-   **2 sessions per day**
-   **15--20 minutes total**

Structure

Home Session → Core activation + compression\
Gym Session → Support + L-sit skill

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# HOME SESSION (8--10 min)

## 1 Dead Bug

![Dead
Bug](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/0e/Dead_bug_exercise.png)

Purpose

-   Activate deep core
-   Stabilize pelvis
-   Protect lower back

Execution

1.  Lie on floor
2.  Press lower back into ground
3.  Extend opposite arm and leg

Tempo

2 sec extend\
1 sec pause\
2 sec return

Breathing

Exhale during extension\
Inhale during return

Volume

**3 × 8 reps**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2 Hollow Hold

![Hollow
Hold](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/3c/Hollow_body_hold.png)

Purpose

-   Anti‑extension core
-   Pull‑up body stability
-   Gymnastics core foundation

Execution cues

-   Lower back pressed to floor
-   Pelvis slightly posterior tilt
-   Rib cage down

Breathing

Small relaxed breaths

Volume

**3 × 30 sec**

Progression goal

45 sec hold

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3 Seated Compression Lift

![Compression
Lift](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/6e/Seated_leg_lift_exercise.png)

Purpose

-   Key strength for **L-sit**
-   Hip flexor development
-   Active leg compression

Setup

Sit upright\
Legs straight\
Hands beside hips\
Lean slightly forward

Movement

Lift both legs simultaneously

Even **1--5 cm lift counts**.

Regression

Single‑leg lifts if necessary

Technique cues

Knees locked\
Toes pointed\
Pause 1 sec at top

Breathing

Exhale while lifting\
Inhale while lowering

Volume

**4 × 10 reps**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# GYM SESSION (After Pull-up Training)

## 1 Support Hold

![Support
Hold](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/58/Dip_bar_support_hold.png)

Purpose

-   Shoulder depression strength
-   Straight arm support
-   L‑sit foundation

Execution

Lock elbows\
Push shoulders down\
Lift body tall

Cue

**Push the bars down**

Breathing

Normal breathing

Volume

**4 × 20 sec**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2 Tuck L‑Sit

![Tuck
L-Sit](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/12/Tuck_L-sit.png)

Purpose

-   Integrate core compression with support
-   Direct L‑sit progression

Execution

Push up on bars\
Bring knees to chest\
Feet off floor

Technique cues

Shoulders depressed\
Elbows locked\
Knees tight to chest

Breathing

Small breaths

Volume

**4 × 10 sec hold**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3 L‑Sit Attempts

Attempt full L‑sit at the end of training.

Even minimal lift counts.

Volume

**3--5 attempts**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**15--20 minutes/day**

# Weekly Progress Targets

Week 1--2

Compression lift\
Legs lift 3 cm

Week 3--4

Tuck L‑sit\
15 sec hold

Week 5

Single‑leg L‑sit

Week 6

Full L‑sit\
3--5 sec

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Key Technique Rules

Always remember

-   Shoulders depressed
-   Elbows locked
-   Slight forward lean

Mental cue

Push bars down\
Lean forward

This makes lifting legs easier.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Execution Rules

Train **2 sessions daily**\
Never train to failure\
Keep movements controlled\
Prioritize technique

Consistent training usually unlocks **L‑sit in 3--4 weeks**.
