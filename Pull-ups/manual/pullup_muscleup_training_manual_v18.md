# 3. Weekly Structure

Training frequency:

Pull-up training 3--4 times per week

  Day         Focus
  ----------- -----------------------------------
  Monday      Volume Day
  Wednesday   Strength Day
  Friday      Speed / Technique

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Monday -- Volume Day

Goal:

Increase total pull-up volume and movement efficiency.

## Warm-up

Shoulder circles -- 20 × 2\
Band pull-aparts -- 20 × 2\
Scapular Pull-up -- 3 × 6\
Active Hang -- 2 × 15s\
Hollow Hold -- 2 × 20s

## Main Training

Pull-up -- 5 × 5\
Explosive Pull-up -- 3 × 2\
L-sit -- 3 × 15s

Total pull-up reps ≈ 24

## Cooldown

Lat stretch -- 30s × 2\
Chest stretch -- 30s × 2\
Rear shoulder stretch -- 30s × 2\
Forearm stretch -- 30s × 2

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Wednesday -- Strength Day

# 8. Plateau Breakthrough System

Used if pull-up progress stalls for 3--4 weeks.

### Density Training

EMOM 10 minutes

3 pull-ups each minute

Total volume ≈ 30 reps

Rule:

Never train to failure.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Cluster Training

Pull-up -- 4 × (2 + 2 + 2)

Structure:

2 reps\
Rest 20 seconds\
2 reps\
Rest 20 seconds\
2 reps

This allows high volume with lower fatigue.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Top Range Strength

Top Hold -- 3 × 10s\
Upper-range Pull-up -- 3 × 5\
Slow Negative -- 3 × 2

Focus:

Strength in final 5 cm of the pull-up.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Elbow Protection System

## Pre-training activation

Finger extension band -- 2 × 20

Purpose:

Balance forearm muscles.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Post-training recovery

Forearm stretch -- 30s × 2\
Lat stretch -- 30s × 2

Optional:

Massage ball release -- 2 minutes

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Load rules

Pull-ups should **never reach failure**.

Always leave:

1--2 reps in reserve.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Pain rule

If elbow pain lasts more than 48 hours:

Reduce pull-up volume by 30%.

Pause explosive pull-ups until pain disappears.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
