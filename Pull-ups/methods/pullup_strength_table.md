# Pull-up Strength Progression Table

## Core Rule

Pull-up training sets should be:

70--85% of max

This zone provides: - Best neural adaptation - Better recovery - Higher
training frequency

## Strength Table

  Max Pull-up   Training Reps   Suggested Structure
  ------------- --------------- ---------------------
  4             3               6×3
  5             3--4            6×3 / 5×4
  6             4--5            5×5
  7             5               6×5
  8             5--6            5×6
  9             6               6×6
  10            6--7            5×7

## Example (Current Level)

Max = 6

Recommended training:

5×5 Pull-ups

Total reps: 25

## When to Increase Difficulty

Increase when: - All sets feel stable - Last set still has 1 rep in
reserve

Example:

5×5 becomes easy → upgrade to 5×6

## Testing Max

Test only if:

-   Two weeks of stable training
-   Training sets feel easier

Test frequency:

Every 2 weeks

## Weekly Volume Guidelines

Beginner: 30--40\
Intermediate: 40--60\
Advanced: 60--80

Recommended for current level:

50--60 per week
